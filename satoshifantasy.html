Summary: "Distributed Engineered Autonomous Agents: Satoshi Fantasy" presents Satoshi Fantasy, a distributed autonomous fantasy football game inspired by the creator of Bitcoin, Satoshi Nakamoto. The paper discusses the challenges of decentralized exchanges and proposes a solution using a proof-of-skill blockchain.

Satoshi Fantasy uses Fantasybits as tokens based on fantasy football points. These bits represent a player's skills and are awarded based on weekly projections. The system employs a proof-of-skill blockchain for consensus, and players mine their own fantasy names into existence. The paper explains the mechanics of projections, skill rankings, data transitions, and time transitions.
